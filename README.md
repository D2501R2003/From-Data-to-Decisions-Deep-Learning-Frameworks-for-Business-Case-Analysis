##  Business Problem

An Audiobooks company wants to **predict whether a customer will buy another audiobook** in the near future.  

Accurate predictions allow the business to:
- Target high-value customers with personalized offers
- Reduce marketing waste on low-conversion users
- Improve customer retention and lifetime value
- Optimize inventory and content recommendations

This is a **binary classification** problem solved with a **Deep Neural Network** built in TensorFlow/Keras.
